<p>There’s this climatic phenomenon called <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Calima_(Saharan_sand)">Calima</a>, where winds from Sahara bring over dust particles into the atmosphere.</p>

<p>This affects the Canary Islands, and the sky remains dusty for days.</p>

<p>Unpredictable, not good for your health, but manageable.</p>

<p>Nonetheless, Tenerife is a good spot for a meetup 🥃</p>
