Long time no see!. It must been a month, at least, since my last post. I've come across <a title="Hidden Wallpapers" href="http://cultofmac.com.feedsportal.com/c/33797/f/606249/s/23455088/l/0L0Scultofmac0N0C1895320Cheres0Ehow0Eto0Eunlock0E430Eamazing0Ewallpapers0Ehidden0Ein0Eos0Ex0Emountain0Elion0C/story01.htm" rel="nofollow">this</a> article... and thought it would be a good idea to store this somewhere, so i don't loose track.

It's simple. Just open this folder:

`/System/Library/Frameworks/ScreenSaver.Framework/Versions/A/Resources/Default Collections/`

You'll find about 147 MB of high definition wallpapers, that are already bundled in your Mac, and nobody told you. They're retina-sized (3200x3000), so if you're using a Macbook Pro with Retina Display.. it will definitely look awesome.
