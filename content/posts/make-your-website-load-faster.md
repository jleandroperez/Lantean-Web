I had to optimize a website, a couple days ago. My goal was to lower the time required to load the website. So... i spent quite some time doing a quick research... and found this <a title="ImageOptim" href="http://imageoptim.com/" rel="nofollow">awesome tool</a>, which by the way is free!.

<a href="/make-your-website-load-faster/screen-shot-2013-01-21-at-11-08-54-am/" rel="attachment wp-att-584"><img class="size-full wp-image-584 aligncenter" alt="Screen Shot 2013-01-21 at 11.08.54 AM" src="/wp-content/uploads/2013/01/Screen-Shot-2013-01-21-at-11.08.54-AM.png" width="413" height="319" /></a>

It's called ImageOptim. You simply drag &amp; drop the assets you'd like to compress, and the guy will do the rest. I've been able to compress most of the images i've got, to 50% of their original size.

So now... my websites load super fast. If you're running Windows (or Linux)... you could try <a title="Imagemagick" href="http://www.imagemagick.org/script/index.php" rel="nofollow">ImageMagick</a>. Which, by the way, is excellent as well.
