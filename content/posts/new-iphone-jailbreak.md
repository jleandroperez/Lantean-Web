Today, a new iPhone Jailbreak solution, has been announced to be already on its way. iOS 6.0 and 6.1 beta 4 have been both jailbroken, and the team is waiting for iOS 6.1 final release to launch the goodies!.

If you have a previous iOS version, and you still wanna jailbreak your device, you should head to <a href="http://www.jailbreakmatrix.com/" rel="nofollow">this</a> site. They have a nice archive of every JB solution that got released.

By the way, Jailbreaking an iOS device is completely legal. However, <strong>downloading illegal copies of iOS Apps is not cool</strong>, and we recommend you do not engage in those activities.

Support the developers..!!
