<p>What nobody tells you about Stonehenge is that there’s a freeway about 200 meters away. The cars are SO CLOSE!</p>

<p>Last but not least.. there’s a fence surrounding the stones. You cannot approach any closer than 100m, give or take.</p>

<p>Reminds me a bit of my expectations toward seeing the Mona Lisa. There were just SO MANY people, it was like being in a Metallica concert, trying to reach the first row.</p>
